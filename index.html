What is P-EV

Platelet-derived Extracellular Vesicles (P-EV) are derived from your blood (autologous), containing healing factors, such as bio-active proteins and growth factors. These are vital for tissue regeneration and repair. Platelets, once thought to be responsible for only clotting, have been proven to be a reservoir of these vital healing components. With advanced techniques, we are able to concentrate these regenerative healing factors in our current Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) laboratory.
